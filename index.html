Hello friends, and welcome to the East Tennessee Relic & Coin Hunters Club. We are a metal detecting community located in Knoxville, Tennessee and the surrounding areas. Our club is dedicated to preserving history one or more pieces at a time. We have been featured on the late news as well as the ABC Morning News. If you are interested in joining our club, ask us about a membership form.
