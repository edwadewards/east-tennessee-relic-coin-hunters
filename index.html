Hello friends, and welcome to the East Tennessee Relic & Coin Hunters Club. We are a metal detecting community located in Knoxville, Tennessee and the surrounding areas. Our club has a passion for treasure hunting and a vast expertise in the machines we hunt with. We have been featured on the late news as well as the ABC Morning News. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned veteran, this is the spot for you. We love to see our club grow. We have regular meetings and hunts so if you are interested in joining our club, ask us about a membership form.
